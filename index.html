Mission & Vision

MPC exists to give producers in Manitoba a home base — not just in name, but in practice.

Mission

The Manitoba Producer Council supports and elevates the creators who shape our province’s music. We help producers connect, learn, and grow by offering community, resources, and opportunities tailored to their craft.

Our mission is to strengthen Manitoba’s producer community and create space where creativity, collaboration, and innovation can thrive.

Vision

A Manitoba where every producer — from emerging to established — has access to a vibrant network, meaningful opportunities, and pathways to develop their sound and career.

We see producers recognized as essential contributors to the province’s creative landscape, not an afterthought.

Core Values

How MPC operates and what it stands on.

  • Inclusivity – All genres, all backgrounds, all experience levels.
  • Empowerment – Tools, knowledge, and support that actually move producers forward.
  • Collaboration – Connecting producers with each other and with artists, studios, and opportunities.
  • Innovation – Encouraging new ideas, new sounds, and new ways to work.
  • Community – A space where producers can grow together instead of competing alone.

About MPC

A council built for producers first — not as a tagline, but as a practice.

Who We Are

The Manitoba Producer Council (MPC) is a community-focused initiative dedicated to supporting producers across the province. We represent beatmakers, electronic artists, engineers, self-producing musicians, and creators of all kinds who work behind the music.

The goal is simple: build a serious, stable environment where producers can improve their craft, collaborate without ego, and access meaningful opportunities.

What We Do

  • Education & Resources – Workshops, guides, and shared knowledge from working producers.
  • Community & Networking – Events, listening sessions, and spaces to connect.
  • Opportunity Creation – Showcases, placements, collaborations, and strategic links.
  • Support & Guidance – Help navigating tools, funding routes, and the business side.

Why Become a Member?

Joining MPC puts you in a community that understands the work you do and respects the craft. It’s not about clout — it’s about better records, better infrastructure, and better support for the people behind the sound.
